Le Marche

Villa Bastilicata Grande

Perfect place for private, family and corporate rest in Le Marche region, with best nature views. Bastilicata Grande Villa also includes a spa center and hot tub. Guests can enjoy an outdoor pool and a restaurant catering. There is a sitting area, a dining area and a kitchen. A flat-screen TV with satellite channels is featured.

You can play tennis at the property, and the area is popular for windsurfing and canoeing.  The villa is located 4-minute walk from the beach. Featuring free WiFi and garden views and lots of sitting area, there are lot of things.

You can play ping-pong at the property, and the area is popular for golfing. Pisa is 17 miles from the villa. The nearest airport is Galileo Galilei Airport, 17 miles from the property.

The villa is located in the culturally fascinating place and it’s open during all 4 seasons for private, family and corporate rest.

Details

  • Adults: 8
  • Amenities: Air conditioning, Beachfront, Dining area, Flat-screen TV, Free parking, Free WiFi, Outdoor pool
  • View: Beachfront, Outdoor pool
  • Size: 180m²
  • Bed Type: 2 Queen beds, 3 Tween beds, 1 Sofa
  • Categories: Le Marche
